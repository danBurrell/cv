I am a Web Developer with a background in mathematics and education at secondary and tertiary levels. I am also proficient in the R, Python3 and Julia languages in the context of data science, numerical computing and statistical modeling. My knowledge of the Python3 language extends beyond data science to the wider web and software development arenas, including back-end web development with Django. I also dabble in Haskell.
